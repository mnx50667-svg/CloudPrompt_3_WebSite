Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

CloudPrompt is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

The short version: Your prompts are stored in YOUR Google Drive. We never see or access your data.

What We Don't Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. Here's what we do NOT collect:

  • Your prompts or saved content
  • Your Google Drive files (beyond the CloudPrompt folder)
  • Your browsing history or activity
  • Personal information beyond what Google OAuth provides
  • Analytics or tracking data

How Your Data Is Stored

All your prompts are stored in your personal Google Drive:

  • • Location: A folder called "CloudPrompt" in your Google Drive root
  • • Format: JSON file (prompts.json) containing all your prompts
  • • Encryption: Protected by Google Drive's security infrastructure
  • • Control: You can access, modify, export, or delete your data anytime

We do not store your prompts on our servers. Your data stays entirely within your Google account.

Google OAuth Permissions

When you connect CloudPrompt, we request minimal permissions:

drive.file

This scope allows us to create and manage only files that CloudPrompt creates. We cannot see, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive.

You can disconnect CloudPrompt from your Google account at any time through Settings or your Google Account permissions page.

Local Storage

CloudPrompt uses your browser's local storage for:

  • • Caching: Storing prompts locally for faster access and offline use
  • • Preferences: Saving your settings (theme, default category, etc.)
  • • Authentication: Storing your OAuth token securely

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared through Settings → Clear Cache.

Website Analytics

Our website (cloudprompt.app) uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors find and use our site. This includes:

  • • Page views and traffic sources
  • • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • • Browser and device information

This data is anonymous and helps us improve the website. The Chrome extension itself does not collect analytics.

Third-Party Services

CloudPrompt integrates with:

  • • Google OAuth: For secure authentication
  • • Google Drive API: For storing your prompts

Your use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • HTTPS for all communications
  • Minimal permissions (drive.file scope only)
  • No server-side storage of your prompts

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • • Access: View all your prompts in the extension or directly in Google Drive
  • • Export: Download your data as JSON or CSV anytime
  • • Delete: Remove individual prompts or your entire library
  • • Disconnect: Revoke CloudPrompt's access to your Google account

If you uninstall CloudPrompt, your data remains in your Google Drive. You can delete it manually or keep it for future use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, if appropriate, through the extension or email.
